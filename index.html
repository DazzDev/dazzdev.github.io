About me

My name is Dazz and I'm a 20 year old Java developer, with special interest in the Bukkit and Spigot APIs for Minecraft servers.

I've been in contact with programming languages for 6 years, and with the development of Minecraft plugins for 4 years, having learned the Java language, Maven, SQL and their particularities in the process.

Over the past year, I started selling custom made plugins, gaining experience in communicating with customers. I also launched a Minecraft server, with plugins I designed myself, improving my server owning skills as well as putting some of my code in practice.

I'm additionally keen on website creation, music, movies, videogames, and cryptocurrencies.
